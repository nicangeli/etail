    EXCESS INVENTORY.

    CONSUMER ELECTRONICS.



  • EXCESS INVENTORY

    We provide a trusted exit channel for the disposal of excess inventory

    Surplus, end-of-line and returned inventory

    Audio-visual consumer electronics
     
    We provide a trusted disposition channel for problem inventory to brands and distributors in the consumer electronics sector (CE).
     
    We help to move high volume, excess, end-of-line (EOL) and returned inventory, through a range of business and consumer distribution channels.
     
    Dealing with problem inventory can be a huge challenge. We have over 5 years experience helping brands on this problem and have a route to market like few other disposition partners.
     
    There are numerous commercial factors that need to be considered. Not only the swift realisation of assets, but the channel management of inventory and the protection of brand identity.
     

    With our experience, we have helped  to distribute over half a million audio-visual accessories, ranging from a single pallet to multiple lorry-loads, with the aid of our distribution centres in London and Portsmouth.

     

    We're always keen to build relationships, so please do not hesitate to connect with us below.

    FROM PALLETS TO CONTAINERS.



  • Case Studies

    Here are some examples of how we've been able to help

    Unpredictable changes in technology

    New lightning connector for iPhone
     
    With the introduction of the new lightning connector we found a number of brands with sudden overstocks of 30 pin docking stations, who needed to find a quick route to market.
     
    We were able to redistribute multiple container loads of docking stations within a short space of time through our innovative approaches and various distribution channels.

    Limited compatibility 

    Product branded and marketed for a very specific purpose
     
    Many brands have come to us with large overstocks of a particular accessory that was marketed for a very specific purpose.
     
    Through our innovative approaches we were were able to re-market the products, helping to move multiple lorry loads of inventory whose value may never otherwise have been realised.
